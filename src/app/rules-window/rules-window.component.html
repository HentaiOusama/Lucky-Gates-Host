How To Play?

Creating and Joining Games


Players can create a new Game by clicking on "New Game" in the main menu.

Once created, the game will enter the player gathering phase that lasts for {{playerGatheringDuration}} secs.

To join a game, players can click on "Join Game" and join a game based on game Id.

For each game, at least {{minPlayers}} players should join the game.

There can be a maximum of {{maxPlayers}} players in each game.

There are two ways for the game to start.
  • Min. no. of players have joined the game and the game creator clicks on begin early.
  • The minimum no. of players has joined the game and the timer runs out.
Once the game starts, one ticket gets deducted from the account of each player.

In case the game is canceled due to lack of players, then the ticket is refunded back.

Game Play

Once the game starts, each player gets to play their turn one after another.

The turns order is based upon the order in which the player joined the game.

In each turn: -
  1. There are {{doorCount}} doors in front of the players numbered 1 to {{doorCount}}.
  2. {{doorPoints}} points are hidden behind doors in random order.
  3. First, the player playing the turn has to pick one door. Let's call it door A.
  4. Once selected, the game will open {{openDoorCount}} other doors, excluding door A.
  5. These opened doors can have only {{openDoorPoints}} points.
  6. The player is then provided with a choice of switching or not switching the door.
  7. If the player decides to switch the door, they must select a new door other than the opened door and door A. This newly selected door becomes their final door.
  8. If the player decides not to switch the door, then door A becomes their final door.
  9. Next, the game will reveal the points behind their final door which will get added to their total points.
Once every player has played their turn, all players with LEAST points are then removed from the game.

If all players have the same points, NO one is removed from the game.

Rest of the players will enter the next round, and once again play their turn.

This cycle continues, until only one player is left.

The last remaining player is the winner of the game, and they will receive the winning reward.
